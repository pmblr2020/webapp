About Unisys Inteliserve

An intelligent workspace platform for frictionless user experiences and superior field services

The InteliServe™ platform is powered by automation and artificial intelligence (AI), advanced analytics, machine learning, robotic process automation (RPA), workforce optimization and can integrate with Unisys biometric authentication. Originally launched as a managed service desk offering, the InteliServe™ application has now expanded to all services including field engineering services and smart workspaces. The platform can also extend into business operations such as human resources, finance and legal.
